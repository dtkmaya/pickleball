Community Guidelines

Respect and Decency

  • Treat all users with respect and basic decency.
  • No harassment, bullying, or intimidation.
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or offensive language.
  • No sharing of explicit content or inappropriate material.

Consequences of Violations

Violating these guidelines can result in the following actions:

  • Warning: A warning will be issued for minor infractions.
  • Temporary Ban: Users may be temporarily banned from chat or from the platform for repeated or severe violations.
  • Permanent Ban: In cases of extreme or repeated misconduct, users may be permanently banned from Pickleheads.

Contacting Support

If you have questions or need to report an issue directly, please talk to our support team.

Updates to Guidelines

These guidelines may be updated from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes.

Thank you for being a part of the LZGroup community. Let's work together to create a positive and enjoyable space for everyone!
